# MySellerCentral Product Documentation

## Overview

MySellerCentral is a comprehensive multi-channel e-commerce management platform that provides AI-powered analytics, content generation, advertising optimization, and business intelligence for marketplace sellers. As a trusted partner with major marketplaces like Amazon and Walmart, MySellerCentral offers deep integration with marketplace APIs to deliver actionable insights and automated workflows.

## Product Philosophy

MySellerCentral is built on three core pillars:

1. **Deep API Integration**: Trusted partner status with Amazon, Walmart, and other major marketplaces enables access to comprehensive seller data
2. **AI-Powered Intelligence**: Advanced AI/ML capabilities for content generation, image analysis, and predictive analytics
3. **Unified Multi-Channel Management**: Single platform to manage sales, inventory, content, and advertising across all marketplaces

### Marketing & Advertising Management

#### Overview

Comprehensive advertising management platform specifically designed for Amazon Advertising, with support for all campaign types (Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Display). Provides campaign creation, optimization, and detailed performance analytics with multiple reporting levels.

**Key Metrics (Top-Level)**

- **Total Sales**: Aggregate sales across all campaigns
- **Total Ad Sales**: Sales attributed to advertising
- **Total Ad Spend**: Total advertising expenditure
- **Impressions**: Total ad impressions served
- **Clicks**: Total clicks on advertisements
- **ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sale)**: Ad Spend / Ad Sales
- **TACOS (Total Advertising Cost of Sale)**: Ad Spend / Total Sales
- **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)**: Ad Sales / Ad Spend

- **CTR (Click-Through Rate)**: Clicks / Impressions
- **Orders**: Total orders from ads

- Category Performance breakdown (by Ad Sales or other metrics)

## Glossary of Terms

**ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sale)**: The ratio of ad spend to ad sales, expressed as a percentage. Lower is generally better. Formula: (Ad Spend / Ad Sales) × 100

**TACOS (Total Advertising Cost of Sale)**: The ratio of ad spend to total sales (both organic and paid), expressed as a percentage. Formula: (Ad Spend / Total Sales) × 100

**ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)**: The amount of revenue generated for every dollar spent on advertising. Higher is better. Formula: Ad Sales / Ad Spend

**CPC (Cost Per Click)**: The average amount paid for each click on an advertisement. Formula: Ad Spend / Clicks

**CTR (Click-Through Rate)**: The percentage of impressions that result in clicks. Formula: (Clicks / Impressions) × 100

**Buy Box**: The featured offer on an Amazon product detail page where customers can add items to their cart

**ASIN (Amazon Standard Identification Number)**: A unique identifier assigned to products on Amazon

**SP-API (Selling Partner API)**: Amazon's API for programmatic access to seller data and operations

**FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon)**: Amazon's fulfillment service where they store, pack, and ship products

**FBM (Fulfillment by Merchant)**: Seller-fulfilled orders where the seller handles storage and shipping

**A+ Content**: Enhanced brand content on Amazon product pages with images, comparison charts, and formatted text

**SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)**: A unique identifier for each distinct product and service that can be purchased

**ONDC (Open Network for Digital Commerce)**: India's government-backed open e-commerce network

**GST (Goods and Services Tax)**: India's value-added tax on goods and services

**PAN (Permanent Account Number)**: A unique identification number for taxpayers in India

## FAQ

**Q: How often is data refreshed from marketplaces?**

A: Order data is updated in real-time. Performance metrics refresh hourly. Advertising data updates every hour for most metrics, with some attribution data updating daily. ONDC orders are synced in real-time.

**Q: Can I connect multiple Amazon seller accounts?**

A: Yes, with add-on pricing. Base plans include single marketplace access. Additional marketplaces cost ₹299/month (India) or $5/month (International) each.

**Q: How do AI Agent tokens work?**

A: Each AI agent use consumes tokens based on the per-use pricing (₹10-₹50 depending on agent). Purchase tokens separately from your subscription. Tokens are valid for 6 months from purchase.

**Q: Which AI Agents work with ONDC?**

A: All agents except Amazon-specific ones work with ONDC: Smart Listing, Lifestyle Image Generator, Infographic Generator, Color Variants Generator, and Banner Image Generator. Text/Image Grading, A+ Content, and Competition Alerts are Amazon-only.

**Q: What's the ONDC pricing model?**

A: ONDC charges 5% per order with free onboarding. No subscription required for ONDC-only sellers. AI Agents are available separately on pay-as-you-go basis.

**Q: Is my marketplace API data secure?**

A: Yes, all API credentials are encrypted at rest and in transit. We use OAuth 2.0 for authentication and never store your marketplace passwords. Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II certified.

**Q: Can I export my data?**

A: Yes, all reports can be exported to CSV, Excel, or PDF formats.

**Q: How does ONDC smart onboarding work?**

A: Upload your GST certificate, PAN card, and bank account details. Our system automatically verifies the information and activates your ONDC seller account within minutes. AI then helps auto-populate product listings with ONDC best practices.

**Q: Which logistics partners are integrated for ONDC?**

A: We have integrated DTDC and Ekart for ONDC order fulfillment. Automatic shipping label generation and tracking updates are included.

**Q: Do you support multi-currency reporting?**

A: Yes, we support all marketplace currencies and provide consolidated reporting in your preferred currency with real-time exchange rates.

**Q: How accurate are the AI-generated listings?**

A: Our AI models are trained on millions of successful marketplace listings and achieve 95%+ accuracy for marketplace compliance. However, we recommend human review before publishing, especially for regulated products.

**Q: Can I use AI Agents without a subscription?**

A: Yes, AI Agents are available as standalone pay-as-you-go services. However, some marketplace-specific agents (Text/Image Grading, A+ Content, Competition Alerts) require connecting your Amazon account.

**Q: How do I interpret "New To Brand" metrics?**

A: New To Brand metrics show customers who haven't purchased from your brand in the past 12 months. This helps measure true customer acquisition vs retention.
